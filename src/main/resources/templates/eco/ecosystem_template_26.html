Asycube 50 & Asycube 80

Asyril SA

Asycube 50 and Asycube 80 are feeders for parts and components from < 0.1 to 10 mm in size.
• Compatible with all kind of parts, including complex geometries
• Precise tuning of flipping amplitude to guarantee gentle handling of highly delicate parts
• Different structured platforms (flat, grooves, holes, …) for optimal parts orientation and simplified robot input
• Swift and straightforward platform change for rapid series changeover
• Easy configuration of new vibration recipes for improved flexibility and scalability
• 3-axis vibration technology to ensure rapid cycle time
• Dedicated Asyril HMI software for simple and intuitive configuration
• Effortless system integration with dedicated plugins
