Hand-E

Robotiq

Description
Hand-E's high accuracy and 50mm parallel stroke make it ideal for precision assembly tasks, while its sealed design ensures reliability even in the most demanding manufacturing conditions. The design of Robotiq Hand-E complies with ISO/TS 15066 standard best practices, featuring maximum force control, rounded edges, self-locking functionalities, and more, making it the optimal gripper for collaborative robots.

Suitable applications
• Pick-and-place
• Inspection
• Assembly
• Machine Tending
