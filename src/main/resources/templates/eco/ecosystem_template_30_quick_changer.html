Quick Changer

OnRobot

Description
> Quick Changer: The Quick Changer enables easy and fast tool changes, handling up to 20kg payload with a low built-in height and weight. It features a patented, reliable, and easy-to-use locking mechanism, with an extra safety locking structure and locking springs, making it ideal for all collaborative robot applications. With the Quick Changer, you'll have the easiest and fastest tool changer, with everything you need included in the box.

> Dual Quick Changer: The Dual Quick Changer allows you to use our end-of-arm tools together in a single cycle. With this innovative solution, you can combine two RG2 grippers or an RG6 gripper with a VG10. Quickly and seamlessly switch between our tools to maximize the utilization of your robots with the Dual Quick Changer.
