3FG15

OnRobot

Description
The 3FG15 three-finger gripper boasts an impressive maximum stroke of 150mm and a sleek design, making it capable of handling multiple processes with ease. It is ideal for gripping a wide range of cylindrical objects from 20mm to 150mm in diameter, making it perfect for machine-tending applications such as CNC lathe machines, insertion tasks, and palletizing.
The 3FG15 offers a strong, stable grip for both form fit (inside the object) and friction fit (external) gripping, providing added flexibility to your operations. Its design automatically centers workpieces, ensuring a strong, stable grip and precise placement in machine chucks.
With a payload capacity of up to 15kg and a gripping force ranging from 10N to 240N, the 3FG15 competes with larger, bulkier, and far less flexible finger grippers, delivering superior performance and versatility.
