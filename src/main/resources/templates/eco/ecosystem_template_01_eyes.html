EYES

OnRobot

Description
The OnRobot Eyes simplifies and makes affordable the deployment of vision in almost any collaborative application. Eyes is perfect for sorting a variety of objects, CNC machine tending with metal parts defined by their outer shape, and numerous other pick-and-place applications where orientation is crucial.

Specifications

With the OnRobot Eyes, you can now easily sort, pick, and place unstructured applications with high reliability using any robot arm. Eyes can be mounted either on the robot wrist or externally, with adjustable positioning to suit your application. It integrates seamlessly with the robot interface and gripper via the OnRobot Quick Changer. Deployment is rapid, ensuring minimal downtime during changeovers to new products or processes. One-picture calibration and part recognition take just minutes, and programming is intuitive and quick. With Eyes' advanced, affordable 2.5D vision, you gain depth perception and the ability to stack parts without added complexity.
