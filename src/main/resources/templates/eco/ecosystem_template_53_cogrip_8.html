CoGrip-8/4

CoRobotics

The CoGRIP gripper enables the handling of various items using four vacuum cups arranged in one or two sections, effectively gripping many shapes and objects. Its flexible design allows for easy adjustment of each cup in three dimensions (X/Y/Z).
• Fast assembly and connection
• Quick adjustment of spacing and height of the vacuum cups in a few minutes
• Reduction of tool change costs
• Capable of gripping objects of any shape through external or internal handles
• Grips elements with different shapes and weights
• Programming completed in just a few minutes
