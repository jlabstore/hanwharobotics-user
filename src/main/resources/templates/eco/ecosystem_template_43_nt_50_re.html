NT 50-RS-EN

Hilscher Gesellschaft für Systemautomation mbH

Description
The netTAP 50 is a protocol converter designed for simple conversions, supporting 1-port Real-Time Ethernet, fieldbus, and serial automation protocols. It handles both slave-to-slave and slave-to-master conversions. As a master, it offers full master functionality to a single slave device, facilitating the integration of a single field device into any higher-level network.
The design features a cost-optimized, compact hardware implementation focused on the essential requirements of a protocol converter. Targeting market segments that prioritize cost savings, the netTAP 50 combines a budget-friendly design with extensive conversion capabilities, making it an attractive and versatile gateway.

Specifications

> IP20, DIN rail mountable, single-row
> 24 volts input power
> LED indicators
> Address switches
> Ethernet service port
> Serial port non-isolated
> Operating temperature 0°C - 60°C
