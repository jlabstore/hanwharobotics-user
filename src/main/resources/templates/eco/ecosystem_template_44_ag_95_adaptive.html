AG-95 Adaptive Gripper

DH-Robotics Technology Co.,Ltd

Description
The AG-95 adaptive gripper is designed for multi-purpose gripping applications, capable of handling objects of various sizes and shapes without needing to change the end-of-arm tools. It features a large stroke and adjustable gripping force. Seamless integration with Hanwha collaborative robots ensures ease of use.
With a plug-in installed in Hanwha’s Rodi-X system, users can fully control the gripper, including position and force control, as well as gripping detection. These functionalities enhance the gripping and manipulating capabilities of robots in various scenarios, such as electronics, automobile component handling, and medical equipment. The DH-Robotics AG-95 gripper significantly improves robotic performance across diverse applications.
