HCR_Dresspack_2M/3M

만희기전

HCR_Dresspack Cable management system developed especially for Hanwha Robotics. The system is easy to operate and protects cables and hoses against wear. '2M' fits HCR-3A & HCR-5A, '3M' fits HCR-12A & HCR-14. It is also available in different diameters depending on size of cables or hoses.
HCR_Dresspack_2M/3M is produced by Mahani Electric, utilizing PMA products that specialize in cable protection systems.

Specifications

Features

• Built-in Swivel system eliminates load on hoses and cables = Increased life
• Easy and quick installation with HCR-Dresspack
• Easy modification of system

Material

• Specially formulated polyamide 6or polyamide 12,
• Free from halogens, REACH RoHS compliant
• Temperature range: -40 °C to 90 °C

Characteristics

• Reduce wear to guidance and control elements
• Increase the degree of freedom of robotic installations
• Allow optimal layout of cables along moving elements
• Increase the lifetime of moving installations
• Allow quick and easy installation

호환 수준

Compatibility

• HCR_Dresspack_2M → HCR-3A, HCR-5A
• HCR_Dresspack_3M → HCR-12A, HCR-14

What's in the box:

• Conduit
• Holders
• Swivel and fitting
