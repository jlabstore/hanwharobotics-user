Hand-E

Robotiq

Description
Hand-E’s high accuracy and 50 mm parallel stroke make it perfect for precision assembly tasks, while its sealed design ensures reliability in the toughest manufacturing conditions. The design of Robotiq Hand-E adheres to the ISO/TS 15066 standard best practices—maximum force, rounded edges, self-locking functionalities, and other features—making it the gripper for collaborative robots.
