Vacuum Gripper Unit ZXP-X1(Compatible with HCR-3A)

SMC Korea, Co.,Ltd

Description
Vacuum Gripper Unit for Collaborate Robots.
Compatible with HCR-3A
* Special design for HCR-5A, HCR-12A, and HCR-14 in process
Operates by simply connecting one air supply tube and M8 electrical wiring connector.
Features an integrated vacuum ejector, air supply/release valve, pressure sensor, and suction cups.
